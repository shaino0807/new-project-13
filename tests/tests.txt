## Test 8 - Ranking refresh uses a completed evidence-gated snapshot
Viewport: desktop
Description: Verifies quota, authorization, maintenance, or upstream failures do not break existing features.

Steps:
1. Navigate to the app without entering a stock.
2. Click the homepage doorway card 股票深度頁, then scroll to 小資價值雷達 where the 搜尋排行榜 button and ranking tabs are located.
3. Observe the ranking snapshot status and click 搜尋排行榜 once. Wait until the job ID has been created and at least the company directory or first persisted batch status is visible; do not wait for all 1,984 stocks in this browser test.
4. Switch between 最被低估, 最被高估, 現金流最佳, and 增長最佳 ranking tabs.

Expected: One refresh creates a versioned persistent background job before external directory work, then loads the complete TWSE and TPEx company directory while keeping emerging stocks in a separate scope. The directory is not marked ready unless TWSE has at least 900 companies, TPEx has at least 700, and the combined listed/OTC scope has at least 1,700; a partial 891-stock TPEx-only response remains retryable and must not start scoring. It advances four-stock resumable batches with an 18-second per-stock ceiling, stores every successful stock in persistent common-feature batches, and visibly reports phase/processed/total/scored/failed counts. A slow stock is queued for retry instead of making the whole request exceed the gateway timeout. The browser remains usable and the latest completed snapshot stays visible while the full-market job continues. Minimum evidence is actively completed from request-deduplicated official financial, monthly-revenue, and TWSE/TPEx valuation caches instead of spending FinMind calls on repeated authorization failures. Reloading or a transient network failure reuses the same job ID for up to seven days, retries only unresolved stocks, and continues missing batches. A new snapshot is published only after at least 95% of the full requested scope has persisted usable common features and every displayed item has a quote date, score, source, reason, and invalidation condition. Switching ranking tabs reads snapshots and does not rescore or require a stock search.
