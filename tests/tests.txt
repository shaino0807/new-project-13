## Test 10 - Dino swing workflow is customer-facing and skill-aligned
Viewport: desktop and mobile
Description: Verifies Dino focuses on usable customer output rather than internal calculation steps.

Steps:
1. Navigate to the app.
2. Click the homepage Dino K棒判斷策略 doorway card or arrow into the deep workspace.
3. Click the Dino K棒判斷策略 tab if it is not already active.
4. Wait for macro/news, 1-3 month screener, thematic tables, and source panel to load.
5. Select at least one candidate stock or enter 2330, 2454 manually.
6. Click 進入深度流程.
7. Wait for the deep workflow result.
8. Return to 股票深度頁 and run 2330.

Expected: The internal workflow explainer and 重新產生候選 button are not visible to normal users. The thematic block shows five tables with readable business/theme notes and clear units for institutional net buy. The source panel shows full-market universe count plus the scored seed count. Deep analysis requires selected or manually entered stock codes and does not break the original stock analysis page.
