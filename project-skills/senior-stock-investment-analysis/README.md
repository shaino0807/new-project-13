# Senior Stock Investment Analysis

This Codex skill creates comprehensive equity research reports for public companies. It is designed for senior stock research workflows that require five complete fiscal years plus TTM financial data, valuation benchmarking, competitive moat assessment, management review, risk analysis, and a final buy, hold, or sell rating.

## When To Use

Use this skill for requests such as:

- "Write a comprehensive investment analysis report for Microsoft (MSFT)."
- "扮演資深股票研究分析師，分析台積電 (TSM)。"
- "Compare current valuation against five-year averages, industry averages, and competitors."

## Required Inputs

At minimum, provide:

- Company name
- Ticker symbol
- Three direct competitors

For the most reliable report, also provide the current share price date, fiscal year-end, industry peer group, and any preferred data source. If live research is required, the assistant should verify current market data and cite sources.

## Output

The report includes:

- Executive summary and rating
- Income statement, balance sheet, and cash flow analysis
- Valuation multiples versus history, industry, and peers
- Business model and moat review
- Growth strategy and TAM discussion
- Management, governance, and capital allocation review
- Company-specific and systematic risks
- Final investment recommendation

## Notes

The skill supports investment research and education. It does not replace personalized financial advice, tax advice, legal advice, or suitability assessment.
